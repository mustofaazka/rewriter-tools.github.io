Contact for support - Rewriter.Tools

In case of questions or suggestions.

    • How to check if double rewriting/uniqueness works?

    • Immediately after registration, you have several thousand characters (double rewriting / uniqueness check). Therefore, do not pay for additional symbols until you are completely satisfied with the quality of the text (there is no tool for a refund in the service).

    • Was a payment made, but the symbols were not credited or not all were credited?

    • Payment and enrollment of symbols is fully automatic. Often, funds are debited and symbols are credited simultaneously. There are times when a payment aggregator takes several minutes to verify your payment. Symbols are always credited exactly as much as you paid with enabled or disabled Double rewrite / Uniqueness check points.

    • Why does a message about a large queue or load sometimes appear?

    • A lot of people use the service for free, so at certain times a long queue can form. You are invited to register, since registered users have a separate queue and rewriting is faster. When the number of registered users becomes very large at the same time, an overload error occurs. You can either wait until the load decreases (usually at night), or pay for symbols (maximum priority for rewriting).

    • Why does a cookie error appear when working through the API?

    • The API only works with characters on the account. If your account has no symbols, you will see a cookies error in the API.

    • Write to us at supportsinoni.men
