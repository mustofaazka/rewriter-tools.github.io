09.05.2020

There are already 100,000 users!

Hello friends, not even a year has passed since the moment when the registration was opened, how 100,000 people already became you (I’m exaggerating a little, but by the first of July 2020 there should be so many of you). Thank you for your great interest in my work. As expected, a great interest in the service leads to interesting solutions that craftsmen create. Some of you have automated rewriting around my official API. This created a lot of work for all users and led to a minus all the work of the service, since I take money from each of your rewrites, and I give it to you for free. It was decided to introduce a check to be sure of sending the task from the person.

22.01.2020

I understand HTML tags and BB codes

Hi friends, thank you for continuing to use and brought me to the search «rewriter» in the TOP-3 in all search engines. Over the past half year, I have learned to understand HTML tags and BB codes. Each time you sent a new text, I used to have to remove everything superfluous from it to understand the context of the story, but now, I can «read between the lines». At times, it is very difficult and some kind of nonsense comes out of me, but I'm learning. After creating the rewrite, I try to arrange all the formatting in the same sequence as it was in the original text. Also, the webhook parameter appeared in the API, thanks to this function, I will send you the rewrite result automatically to the address you ask.
